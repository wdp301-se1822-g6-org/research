# Paper 03 Summary

## Citation

**Title:** Customer experiences in the age of artificial intelligence  
**Authors:** Ameen, N., Tarhini, A., Reppel, A., & Anand, A.  
**Year:** 2021  
**Source:** Computers in Human Behavior  
**DOI/Link:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.chb.2020.106548

## Problem

The paper examines customer experience in AI-enabled service environments and identifies factors that shape customer perceptions of AI-based services.

## Method

The study uses a quantitative research model to analyze AI-enabled customer experience factors.

## Dataset

Survey data from customers who have experience with AI-enabled services.

## Evaluation

The study tests relationships among AI-related service factors and customer experience constructs.

## Results

AI can improve customer experience through convenience, personalization, service quality, and trust-related factors.

## Limitations

The study emphasizes general AI customer experience and does not focus specifically on loyalty tier progression.

## Relevance to our topic

This paper provides a theoretical basis for variables such as AI service quality, personalization, convenience, and trust.

## Possible improvement

Future work can apply the AI customer experience model to a specific service system and measure loyalty outcomes such as retention, repeat use, and tier progression.
