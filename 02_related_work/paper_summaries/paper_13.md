# Paper 13 Summary

## Citation

**Title:** Human-AI interaction in E-Commerce: The impact of AI-powered customer service on user experience and decision-making  
**Authors:** Chau, H. K. L., Ngo, T. T. A., Bui, C. T., & Tran, N. P. N.  
**Year:** 2025  
**Source:** Computers in Human Behavior Reports  
**DOI/Link:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.chbr.2025.100710

## Problem

The paper investigates how AI-powered customer service affects user experience and decision-making in e-commerce.

## Method

The study uses a quantitative model based on human-AI interaction and technology adoption perspectives to analyze AI-powered customer service.

## Dataset

Survey data from users who have experience with AI-powered customer service in e-commerce contexts.

## Evaluation

The study evaluates relationships among AI service factors, user experience, satisfaction, continued use, and purchase decision-related outcomes.

## Results

AI-powered customer service can influence user experience, satisfaction, continued AI usage, and purchase decision-making.

## Limitations

The context is e-commerce, so additional research is needed to test the model in other smart service ecosystems.

## Relevance to our topic

This paper supports the link between AI-powered customer service and user experience, especially in digital service environments.

## Possible improvement

Future research can extend the model to loyalty program contexts and test whether AI service experience predicts customer retention and tier progression.
