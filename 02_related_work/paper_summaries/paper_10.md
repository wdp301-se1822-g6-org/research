# Paper 10 Summary

## Citation

**Title:** Communication strategies for multi-tier loyalty programs: The role of progress framing  
**Authors:** Zeng, K. J., Yu, I. Y., Yang, M. X., & Chan, H.  
**Year:** 2022  
**Source:** Tourism Management  
**DOI/Link:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.tourman.2021.104478

## Problem

The paper investigates how communication strategies and progress framing affect customer responses in multi-tier loyalty programs.

## Method

The study examines progress framing in loyalty program communication and analyzes how framing influences customer attitudes and behavior.

## Dataset

Empirical data from customer experiments or survey-based studies related to multi-tier loyalty programs.

## Evaluation

The study evaluates customer responses to different progress framing strategies in multi-tier loyalty contexts.

## Results

Progress framing can influence how customers perceive their movement toward higher loyalty tiers and can affect motivation and behavioral responses.

## Limitations

The study focuses on communication framing and does not examine AI-powered customer service or chatbot support.

## Relevance to our topic

This paper is important for the domain application because the project studies loyalty tier progression, which is closely related to multi-tier loyalty program design.

## Possible improvement

Future research can investigate whether AI chatbots can personalize progress framing and encourage customers to move toward higher loyalty tiers.
