# Paper 05 Summary

## Citation

**Title:** Assessing the impact of AI-chatbot service quality on user e-brand loyalty through chatbot user trust, experience and electronic word of mouth  
**Authors:** Shahzad, M. F., Xu, S., An, X., & Javed, I.  
**Year:** 2024  
**Source:** Journal of Retailing and Consumer Services  
**DOI/Link:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jretconser.2024.103940

## Problem

The paper investigates how AI-chatbot service quality affects e-brand loyalty and the mediating roles of chatbot user trust, user experience, and electronic word of mouth.

## Method

The study uses a quantitative model to test relationships between chatbot service quality and e-brand loyalty.

## Dataset

Survey data from users who interacted with AI chatbots in online service or retail contexts.

## Evaluation

Statistical modeling was used to test direct and indirect effects among chatbot service quality, trust, user experience, eWOM, and loyalty.

## Results

AI-chatbot service quality can positively affect trust, user experience, electronic word of mouth, and e-brand loyalty.

## Limitations

The study focuses on e-brand loyalty, so actual loyalty tier behavior may need further investigation.

## Relevance to our topic

This paper is directly relevant because it connects AI service quality with trust and loyalty.

## Possible improvement

Future research can replace e-brand loyalty with loyalty tier progression, retention, or long-term engagement in a service ecosystem.
