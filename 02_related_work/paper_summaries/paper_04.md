# Paper 04 Summary

## Citation

**Title:** Can AI chatbots help retain customers? An integrative perspective using affordance theory and service-domain logic  
**Authors:** Li, C.-Y., Fang, Y.-H., & Chiang, Y.-H.  
**Year:** 2023  
**Source:** Technological Forecasting and Social Change  
**DOI/Link:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.techfore.2023.122921

## Problem

The paper asks whether AI chatbots can help retain customers and how chatbot affordances influence customer retention.

## Method

The study integrates affordance theory and service-domain logic to build a research model about chatbot-enabled customer retention.

## Dataset

Survey data from users of AI chatbot services, focusing on chatbot affordances and retention-related outcomes.

## Evaluation

Structural equation modeling was used to test relationships among chatbot affordances, customer experience, and retention outcomes.

## Results

AI chatbot affordances such as personalization and interaction can improve customer experience and contribute to customer retention.

## Limitations

The study focuses on retention and may not directly measure loyalty tier upgrades, reward redemption, or accumulated loyalty behavior.

## Relevance to our topic

This is a core paper because it directly links AI chatbots with customer retention.

## Possible improvement

Future research can extend the retention model by adding loyalty program data and tier progression as an outcome.
