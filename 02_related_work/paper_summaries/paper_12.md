# Paper 12 Summary

## Citation

**Title:** Understanding continuance intention toward the use of AI chatbots in customer service among generation Z in Vietnam  
**Authors:** Ngo, T. T. A., Phan, T. Y. N., Nguyen, T. K., Le, N. B. T., Nguyen, N. T. A., & Le, T. T. D.  
**Year:** 2025  
**Source:** Acta Psychologica  
**DOI/Link:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.actpsy.2025.105235

## Problem

The paper examines the factors that influence Generation Z users’ continuance intention toward AI chatbots in customer service in Vietnam.

## Method

The study uses a quantitative research model to examine how system quality, information quality, service quality, enjoyment, customization, interaction, and problem-solving influence attitude, satisfaction, and continuance intention.

## Dataset

Survey data collected from Generation Z users in Vietnam who have experience with AI chatbot customer service.

## Evaluation

The study tests relationships among quality factors, user attitude, satisfaction, and continuance intention using statistical analysis.

## Results

Factors such as service quality, customization, interaction, enjoyment, and problem-solving can influence satisfaction and continuance intention toward AI chatbots.

## Limitations

The study focuses on Gen Z and continuance intention, so the findings may not directly generalize to all customer segments or actual loyalty tier progression.

## Relevance to our topic

This paper is useful because it provides evidence from the Vietnamese context, which is close to the target users of the proposed study.

## Possible improvement

Future research can add behavioral data such as loyalty points, repeat transactions, and tier changes to validate survey-based continuance intention.
