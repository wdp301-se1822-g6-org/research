# Paper 09 Summary

## Citation

**Title:** Three decades of research on loyalty programs: A literature review and future research agenda  
**Authors:** Chen, Y., Mandler, T., & Meyer-Waarden, L.  
**Year:** 2021  
**Source:** Journal of Business Research  
**DOI/Link:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jbusres.2020.10.057

## Problem

The paper reviews the development of loyalty program research and identifies major themes, outcomes, and future research directions.

## Method

The study uses a literature review approach to synthesize three decades of research on loyalty programs.

## Dataset

A collection of prior studies on loyalty programs, customer retention, reward mechanisms, and loyalty outcomes.

## Evaluation

The paper evaluates prior studies by categorizing themes, methods, findings, and research gaps.

## Results

Loyalty programs influence customer retention, repeat purchase, relationship strength, and customer behavior, but program effectiveness depends on reward design, customer perception, and engagement mechanisms.

## Limitations

The paper is a review and does not focus specifically on AI-powered customer service or chatbot-based loyalty systems.

## Relevance to our topic

This paper provides domain knowledge about loyalty programs and helps define loyalty outcomes such as retention, engagement, and tier progression.

## Possible improvement

Future research can connect loyalty program theories with AI-powered customer service and predictive analytics.
