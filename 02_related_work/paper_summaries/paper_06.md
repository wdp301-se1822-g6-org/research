# Paper 06 Summary

## Citation

**Title:** Service chatbots: A systematic review  
**Authors:** Suhaili, S. M., Salim, N., & Jambli, M. N.  
**Year:** 2021  
**Source:** Expert Systems with Applications  
**DOI/Link:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.eswa.2021.115461

## Problem

The paper reviews the development, methods, evaluation, and application domains of service chatbots.

## Method

The study uses a systematic literature review method to collect and analyze prior chatbot studies.

## Dataset

A collection of service chatbot studies selected from academic databases according to systematic review criteria.

## Evaluation

The paper summarizes chatbot methods, domains, datasets, and evaluation metrics used in previous studies.

## Results

Service chatbots have been applied in many domains. Many studies focus on technical performance, while fewer examine broader customer loyalty outcomes.

## Limitations

As a review paper, it does not provide a new empirical dataset or test a specific loyalty model.

## Relevance to our topic

This paper helps build the background section about service chatbots and identify gaps in customer loyalty research.

## Possible improvement

Future research can combine chatbot technical evaluation with user-centered metrics such as satisfaction, trust, and loyalty.
