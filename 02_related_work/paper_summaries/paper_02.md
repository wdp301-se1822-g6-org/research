# Paper 02 Summary

## Citation

**Title:** AI-based chatbots in customer service and their effects on user compliance  
**Authors:** Adam, M., Wessel, M., & Benlian, A.  
**Year:** 2021  
**Source:** Electronic Markets  
**DOI/Link:** https://doi.org/10.1007/s12525-020-00414-7

## Problem

The paper studies how AI-based chatbots in customer service influence user compliance and whether human-like chatbot design affects user behavior.

## Method

The study uses experimental research to examine chatbot design cues, social presence, and user compliance in customer service interactions.

## Dataset

Experimental user data collected from participants interacting with customer service chatbots.

## Evaluation

The authors evaluate how chatbot design cues influence perceived social presence and user compliance behavior.

## Results

Human-like chatbot design cues can increase social presence, which may lead to higher user compliance during customer service interactions.

## Limitations

The study focuses mainly on compliance rather than long-term outcomes such as loyalty, retention, or tier progression.

## Relevance to our topic

This paper is relevant because it explains how chatbot design can influence customer behavior during service interactions.

## Possible improvement

Future research can examine whether social presence also increases customer trust, satisfaction, loyalty, and long-term engagement.
