## 2. Proposed Title

**English title:**  
AI-Powered Customer Service for Improving Customer Loyalty in Smart Service Ecosystems

**Vietnamese title:**  
Ứng dụng AI trong chăm sóc khách hàng nhằm cải thiện lòng trung thành khách hàng trong hệ sinh thái dịch vụ thông minh

## 3. Application Domain

- Customer Service
- Smart Service Ecosystem
- AI Chatbot / AI Customer Support
- Customer Loyalty and Retention

## 4. Problem Statement

In modern service ecosystems, customers expect fast, accurate, personalized, and convenient support. Traditional customer service often depends heavily on human staff, which can lead to slow response time, inconsistent service quality, and limited personalization. AI-powered customer service, especially chatbot-based support, can help answer customer questions, recommend suitable services, explain promotions, support booking processes, and improve customer experience.

However, many existing studies mainly focus on chatbot satisfaction or continued usage intention, while fewer studies examine how AI-powered customer service may influence customer loyalty outcomes such as customer retention, long-term engagement, and loyalty tier progression. Therefore, this study investigates the AI customer service factors that may affect customer loyalty in smart service ecosystems.

## 5. Motivation

Customer loyalty is important because retaining existing customers is usually more cost-effective than acquiring new customers. In smart service systems, AI can be integrated into customer support to improve response speed, service quality, personalization, and decision support. Understanding which AI customer service factors influence loyalty can help businesses design better AI support systems and loyalty programs.

## 6. Target Users

The target users include:

1. Customers who use online service platforms or booking systems.
2. Users who interact with AI chatbots or AI customer support.
3. Students and staff who have experience using digital services.
4. Service providers who want to improve customer support and loyalty.

## 7. Proposed AI Model / Method

The proposed system focuses on integrating an AI-powered customer service chatbot into a smart service platform.

Possible AI methods:

- LLM-based chatbot for customer support.
- Rule-based recommendation for loyalty benefits and promotions.
- Machine learning model for predicting customer loyalty/retention.
- Classification models such as Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, or XGBoost for loyalty prediction.

## 8. System Features

1. Customer account registration and login.
2. AI chatbot for customer service support.
3. FAQ answering and service recommendation.
4. Promotion and loyalty benefit explanation.
5. Customer feedback collection.
6. Customer satisfaction and trust survey.
7. Optional loyalty/retention prediction based on customer behavior.

## 9. Expected Contribution

1. Identify key AI-powered customer service factors affecting customer loyalty.
2. Propose a research model linking AI service quality, personalization, trust, satisfaction, retention, and loyalty.
3. Design a prototype AI customer service system for smart service ecosystems.
4. Provide an evaluation plan using survey data and optional behavioral logs.

## 10. Evaluation Plan

- **Baseline:** Traditional customer service or rule-based chatbot.
- **Metrics for AI/system:** response time, answer usefulness rating, satisfaction score.
- **Metrics for prediction model:** Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-score.
- **User survey:** perceived usefulness, trust, satisfaction, personalization, continuance intention, and loyalty intention.
